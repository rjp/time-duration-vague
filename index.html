time-duration-vague

Be vague about time durations

For example, if a duration is within 10% (capped to 5 minutes) of a nice duration (multiples of 5, 10, 20, 30, 60 minutes, etc.), t-d-v will report it as "nearly N minutes" (if it's below) or "just past N minutes" (if above). A 5% span (capped to 2.5 minutes) will count as the time itself ("N minutes").

Example return values

Seconds Duration
< 30 just now
175 nearly 3 minutes
200 3 and a bit minutes
265 nearly 5 minutes
550 nearly 10 minutes
